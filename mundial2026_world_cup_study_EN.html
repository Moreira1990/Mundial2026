The Adidas Trionda is the official ball of the 2026 World Cup and the most technologically advanced in history. Wind tunnel tested by the Universities of Puget Sound, Tsukuba and Sheffield, its physics clearly favour teams playing with short touch, high rotation and quality set pieces — and disadvantages teams relying on long defensive clearances. 04
The Astrological Dimension
Clear methodology, controlled weight, scientific curiosity
How we determined each nation's sign — methodology
Método: utilizamos a data do documento formal que constitui o estado moderno de cada país (declaração de independência, constituição, tratado de fundação) para determinar o signo solar. Por exemplo, os Netherlands têm o Act of Abjuration de 26 de julho de 1581 — que cai em Leão. Os EUA têm a Declaração de Independence de 4 de julho de 1776 — Câncer. Esta abordagem é documentalmente verificável e historicamente consistente.

Peso no modelo: as variáveis astrológicas representam apenas 12% do peso total do modelo, divididas em 3 sub-dimensões: (1) signo nacional e trânsitos planetários de jun-jul 2026, (2) karma histórico calculado com fórmula matemática (finals perdidas e anos sem título), (3) alinhamento geral dos trânsitos do período. Nenhuma conclusão final depende desta dimensão isolada.

Transparência editorial: incluímos esta dimensão como elemento de inovação e curiosidade. Os resultados do modelo são robustos mesmo removendo completamente a componente astrológica — os Netherlands continuam em 1.º lugar pelas restantes 24 dimensões.
⚠️ As variáveis astrológicas são de carácter especulativo e cultural. Os dados económicos, desportivos, de saúde pública e da bola Trionda são reais e verificáveis. A astrologia entra no modelo como dimensão complementar, não determinante.

How this model was built
Full transparency — so anyone can verify and replicate
01
Real data collection
All economic, sporting and health variables were collected from international primary sources: FIFA, UNDP, IMF, WHO, World Bank, CIES Football Observatory, Deloitte Money League and Transfermarkt. Data from June 2026.
02
Normalisation 0-100
Each variable was normalised on a 0 to 100 scale to allow comparison between dimensions of different natures (GDP in dollars, FIFA ranking in points, life expectancy in years, etc.).
03
Integrated Klement model
We reproduced the model of mathematician Joachim Klement (who correctly predicted the winners of 2014, 2018 and 2022) and integrated it as one of the 5 categories. Its base variables — GDP, population and culture — carry 22% weight in total.
04
Trionda ball physics
Based on wind tunnel studies from the Universities of Puget Sound, Tsukuba and Sheffield, published in Scientific American, BBC Science Focus and Design News, we created a playing style compatibility score with Trionda physics.
05
Astronomical lunar cycles
The moon phases of June and July 2026 were verified at Astronomy.com and Catalina Sky Survey. Each key match was assigned a score based on the lunar phase on that date — a variable not used in any previous World Cup study.
06
National astrology (12% of model)
We used the date of the founding document of each modern state to determine the national sun sign. This variable represents only 12% of total weight and is presented with full transparency regarding its speculative nature. The final result does not change without it.
